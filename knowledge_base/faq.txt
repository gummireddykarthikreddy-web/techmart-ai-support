TechMart Electronics - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be in original packaging.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

A: Premium subscriptions can take up to 24 hours to activate after payment. If it has been longer, please contact support.
